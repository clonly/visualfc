VFCTools for VCExpress and Code::Blocks

download VFCAddin0.80.RC2 and install
download VFCTools and update

For VS2005/VS2008/VCExpress Tools
Command:  	C:\VisualFC\bin\VFCTools.exe
Arguments:	$(ProjectDir)$(ProjectFileName)

For Code::Blocks Tools
Executable:	C:\VisualFC\bin\VFCTools.exe
Parameters:	${PROJECT_DIR}${PROJECT_FILENAME}


WTLAppWizard Tools
Command/Executable:	C:\VisualFC\bin\AppWizard.exe
